Color Facts

Green

Green, the color of life, renewal, nature, and energy, is associated with meanings of growth, harmony, freshness, safety, fertility, and environment. Green is also traditionally associated with money, finances, banking, ambition, greed, jealousy, and wall street.

— Bourncreative Meaning of The Color Green

Red

Red is the color of extremes. It’s the color of passionate love, seduction, violence, danger, anger, and adventure. Our prehistoric ancestors saw red as the color of fire and blood – energy and primal life forces – and most of red’s symbolism today arises from its powerful associations in the past.

— Color Matters The Meanings of Red

Blue

Blue is a popular color that lies between violet and green in the optical spectrum. The term Blue is derived from ME bleu or blewe or from the Old French bleu. Blue color in factories is a mark to indicate the item is to be reparied. Studies show that Weightlifting are able to handle heavier weights in blue gyms.
